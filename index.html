The goal is to break the fruit!

How to play:
The fruit has power, velocity, and height requirements that must be met before it can be broken.
If all of the requirements are met, the fruit will break!

Controls:
The left mouse button is used to move objects. Hold the button to move an object and let go to drop it.
While holding an object, press Shift to raise the object higher and Spacebar to drop it downwards.
Press L to turn the light off and on.
